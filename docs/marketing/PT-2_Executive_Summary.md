# PT-2 Presentation Summary: The New Standard in Table Games Management

## Overview
PT-2 is a modern table games management system designed for complete transparency and accountability. Unlike legacy systems that operate as "black boxes," PT-2 provides verifiable proof for every calculation, financial record, and staff action.

## The Six Strategic Pillars

### 1. Verifiable Theoretical Win
* **Transparency:** Moves beyond hidden calculations to a published formula.
* **Traceability:** Every input (average bet, house edge, duration) is recorded and preserved.
* **Accountability:** Enables auditors and regulators to independently verify house earnings at any time.

### 2. Immutable Financial Records
* **Ledger Accuracy:** Uses accounting principles where records cannot be edited or deleted.
* **Correction Integrity:** Any adjustments are added as new entries, preserving the original audit trail.
* **Security:** Eliminates the "gaps" found in systems where data can be modified after the fact.

### 3. Append-Only Loyalty Ledger
* **Checkbook Style Tracking:** Every point earned or redeemed is a unique, unchangeable line item.
* **Dispute Resolution:** Provides a clear, visible history for player inquiries.
* **Total Visibility:** Shows exactly who triggered an adjustment, when, and why.

### 4. Non-Spoofable Actor Attribution
* **Identity Enforcement:** Every action is tied to a specific staff member's authenticated login.
* **Elimination of Ambiguity:** Prevents shared logins or "acting as" another user.
* **Security:** High-integrity attribution for comp authorizations and rating changes.

### 5. Structured Audit Traceability
* **End-to-End Lifecycle:** Links a player's first bet to the final loyalty accrual and compliance report.
* **Rapid Auditing:** Transforms hours of manual data cross-referencing into instant, queryable chains.
* **Operational Health:** Provides dashboards to monitor the integrity of the data chain across the property.

### 6. Statistical Anomaly Detection
* **Proactive Monitoring:** Automatically flags unusual table performance (drop, win/loss, hold) based on a 7-day rolling baseline.
* **Actionable Alerts:** Generates auditable alerts for supervisors to review and acknowledge.
* **Decision Support:** Enhances human judgment with objective statistical evidence.

## Key Operational Advantages
* **Rating Coverage:** Tracks exactly how much table operating time is actually being monitored.
* **Liability Snapshots:** Daily reporting on the dollar value of outstanding loyalty points.
* **Cross-Property Recognition:** Single player identity across multiple casino locations.
* **Baseline Provenance:** Clearly identifies the source and reliability of opening chip counts.

## The Core Principle
**Function is not enough—proof is required.** PT-2 ensures that every number produced is traceable, attributed, and independently verifiable.

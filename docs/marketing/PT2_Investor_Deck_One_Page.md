# PT-2

## Operational Intelligence & Compliance Overlay for Table Games

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. The Market Problem

Casinos rely on legacy table-game systems that:

-   Are vendor-locked for decades
-   Lack transparency in win/loss calculations
-   Require manual audit reconciliation
-   Provide limited operational intelligence
-   Make switching prohibitively risky

They "just work" --- but they hide inefficiencies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. The Opportunity

Table games generate millions per property annually.

Even small deltas in:

-   Theo accuracy
-   Drop/fill variance
-   Loyalty comp alignment
-   Audit preparation time

Translate into meaningful financial impact.

Casinos do not need replacement systems.

They need visibility.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. The Solution

PT-2 is a non-invasive overlay that runs alongside existing systems, alternatively: "Deployable as an independent operational layer without requiring legacy system replacement."

It delivers:

-   Deterministic shift baselines
-   Transparent win/loss reconciliation
-   Drop & variance detection
-   MTL/CTR traceability
-   Loyalty margin exposure
-   Audit-grade financial lineage

No core system replacement.\
No downtime.\
Low integration risk.

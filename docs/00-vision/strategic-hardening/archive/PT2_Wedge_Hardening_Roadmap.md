# PT-2 Wedge Hardening & Implementation Gap Roadmap

## Purpose

This document aligns business positioning with architectural hardening.

Every claimed economic delta must map to: - A measurable KPI - A
deterministic data pipeline - A defensible calculation model - A
dashboard surface - A quantified ROI hypothesis

If it cannot be measured, it cannot be sold.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# I. WEDGE A --- Theo Integrity

## Business Claim

Surface theoretical win inconsistencies and baseline opacity.

## Hardening Questions

-   Are baseline sources explicitly classified and surfaced?
-   Can legacy-reported theo be compared against PT-2 computed theo?
-   Is drift detection automated?
-   Is telemetry completeness measurable?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# II. WEDGE B --- Compliance Acceleration

## Business Claim

Reduce audit preparation time and internal control friction.

## Hardening Questions

-   Can an auditor trace a financial event end-to-end?
-   Are MTL triggers deterministic?
-   Is session context injection enforced?
-   Are mutation pathways minimal and auditable?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# III. WEDGE C --- Shift Intelligence

## Business Claim

Surface operational anomalies in real time.

## Hardening Questions

-   Are variance thresholds configurable?
-   Is anomaly logic deterministic?
-   Are alerts actionable or noisy?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# IV. WEDGE D --- Loyalty Margin Clarity

## Business Claim

Align theo accrual with reward issuance and profitability exposure.

## Hardening Questions

-   Is loyalty accrual reversible and auditable?
-   Can reward liability snapshots be computed?
-   Are cross-context boundaries enforced?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Litmus Test

If you cannot walk into a property and say:

"We will surface \$X in operational delta within 90 days."

You are still in engineering mode.

When you can say it --- you have a business.

# Vision & Scope (V&S)

**ID Prefix**: `VIS-###`
**Owner**: Product
**Phase**: Inception, Discovery, Evolve

## Purpose

Aligns direction and guards against scope creep with clear problem statements, goals, non-goals, and stakeholder identification.

## What Belongs Here

- **Vision Brief**: One-pager defining the problem and solution approach
- **Success Metrics**: KPIs and measurable outcomes
- **Out-of-Scope List**: Explicit non-goals and deferred features
- **Stakeholder Map**: Who cares and why
- **Market Analysis**: Competitive landscape (if applicable)

## Document Format

```yaml
---
id: VIS-001
title: PT-2 Casino Management Platform Vision
owner: Product
status: Accepted
created: 2025-10-25
last_review: 2025-10-25
---
```
